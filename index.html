Adarsha Shikshan Mandali (ASM) was founded by Late Shri K.B.Veerkar aka Tatyasaheb Veerkar in 1943. Adarsha Shikshan Mandali provides education to more than ten thousand students in aided and un-aided set-ups like Pre-Primary, Primary, High School, and Teacher’s Training College (B.Ed) imparting knowledge through English and Marathi medium. In order to enhance and enrich the educational experience, ASM has established Maths Lab, Computer Lab, Science Lab as well as English Lab and a School Observatory. As a stepping stone towards digital teaching and learning, ASM has developed in house ‘Digital Lessons’ in Maths, Science, Geography and English, which proves to be a rewarding experience for students. ASM’s current endeavour is to instill ‘IT’ spirit in the staff. The teachers and staff have successfully completed ‘MS-CIT’ Certificate course. The administrative system is being computerized to make it a paperless educational society. Right from admissions to annual result reports and physical ability reports system has gone digital. Abhinava Vidyalaya was the first Marathi School in Maharashtra to offer ‘German’ as a foreign language to it’s students. Introduction of Semi English Course and Spoken English Classes have equipped the Marathi Medium Students to cope up with current educational scenario. Abhinava Students always participate enthusiastically in inter-school competitions like singing, elocution, story telling, poetry recitations, drawing, quizzes and various fields of sports and bring laurels to school. Supportive and Encouraging Management, Committed and Enthusiastic teachers and staff, talented students, and co-operative parents are the strong pillars of ASM, taking it on a path of progress. ASM AIIT is an authorised Learning Centre of MKCL
